Website Speed Checker


Website Speed Test Tool can be very helpful to analyze your website's loading speed (how fast your site gets loaded) and optimize it to make it fast. It will also give the complete report about all types of content (e.g., scripts, images, documents, etc.) size present on your site. This online tool is completely free to use.


How to Use?

  • Enter your website link (e.g., https://example.com or example.com)

  • Click on the submit button

  • Select your targated location and then click test.
