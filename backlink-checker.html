Backlink Checker: What is it?

A backlink checker is a tool that lets you look at a website's or a particular page's backlinks. It aids in your comprehension of the websites that connect to your website and how they affect your search engine results.


These resources can assist you in recognizing:

  • How many backlinks there are to your website

  • How well the backlinks are constructed

  • In the links, the anchor text

  • The links' original source

  • The pages with the highest number of backlinks on your website

You may also use backlink checker tools to find low-quality backlinks that should be eliminated because they may be negatively affecting your search engine rankings.
